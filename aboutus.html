Petcare is a full-service pet store specializing in providing pet owners with high-quality pet supplies and services. We provide many products and services, such as pet food, toys, supplies, pet grooming, and pet training.  There is lack of proper pet service in Nepal. Many pet foods are lacking proper nutrition.  Pet care is a caring and compassionate pet care company that provides high-quality services and education to pet parents in Nepal such as vaccination, deworming, grooming and so on. We are committed to ensuring that your pets receive the best possible care and that you, as a pet parent, have the resources you need to keep your furry companion healthy and happy.
